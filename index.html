About Me
I am an Undergraduate student in Institute of Interdisciplinary Information Sciences at Tsinghua University since 09/2015.
My research interest lies in artificial intelligence, computational game-theory, computational sustainablity, and mechainism design. I hope to better understand how AI can address social issues and facilitate economic activities using tools from game theory, optimization, and machine learning.
