Exploration of IoT service security
Investigate the potential security problem in the authentication between the cloud and third-party server. Validate the attack through proof-of-concept experiments. Develop a heuristic-based method to search for vulnerable skills in practice.
Paper Peeking Over the Edge: Spoofing the Cloud Service of SmartHome Assistant Systems
Submitted to WWW 2019

Analysis of Swarm users' share-to-Twitter behavior
Investigate the factors that impact Swarm users' behavior on sharing Swarm check-ins to Twitter. The results can help researchers identify whether their Twitter-collected check-ins can present the user behavior on Swarm.
Paper An Empirical Study of the Usage of the Swarm App's Cross-Site Sharing Feature
Workshop AppLens, UbiComp 2018

WeChat Mini Program: Qingyun Go
Develop the whole server of a Wechat Mini Program, Qingyun Go, which allows users to make check-ins, record emotions, and check the weather. The number of registered users has reached 1480 by June 2018.
Paper LBSLab: A User Data Collection System in Mobile Environments
Workshop MHC, UbiComp 2018

Measurement of the 4G network on high-speed rails
Measure the throughput of MPTCP, TCP with CUBIC and BBR congestion control algorithm in the high-speed environment. Investigate the impact of handoff on these three protocols.

Analysis of Swarm users' behavior in cities
Analyze the user behavior through spatial and temporal features of Swarm check-ins. The results reveal the activity patterns of human behavior and the distinctions of life habits among three cities, Hong Kong, New York City, and San Francisco.
Paper Understanding User Activity Patterns of the Swarm App: A Data-Driven Study
UbiComp 2017, Poster Session

Analysis of Skout users' mobility patterns
Crawl the posts data of more than 1.2 million users from Skout, a location based social network, and construct a global city network to figure out users' mobility patterns among cities on a global scale.
Paper Understanding Skout Users’ Mobility Patterns on a Global Scale: A Data-Driven Study
World Wide Web Journal (WWWJ), 2018
